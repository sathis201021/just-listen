Why I started Just Listen

We've all had those moments — something weighing on you, but nowhere to put it. Friends are busy. Family might judge. Therapy feels like too much. You just want someone to hear you out.

That's why I created Just Listen. A simple, human service where you can talk to a real person who won't interrupt, won't fix, and won't judge. Just listen.

What I believe

Everyone deserves to feel heard. Not every problem needs a solution — sometimes the act of speaking it aloud, to someone who's truly present, is enough.

I'm not here to give you advice or tell you what to do. I'm here to hold space for whatever you need to say. Your pace, your words, your story.

What this is — and isn't

Just Listen is a peer listening service. I am not a licensed therapist, counselor, or medical professional. I won't diagnose conditions, prescribe treatments, or provide clinical advice.

If you're dealing with something that needs professional support, I'll gently encourage you to seek it. But if you just need a calm, caring ear — I'm here for that.

How I show up

Every call is audio-only, private, and unrecorded. I show up on time, I listen fully, and I treat everything you share with respect and confidentiality.

No scripts. No agendas. Just two humans having a conversation — one of them there to listen.
