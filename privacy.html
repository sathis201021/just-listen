Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Just Listen, your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

When you book a conversation, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment provider — we do not store card details)
  • Your preferred session time and duration

What We Don't Collect

We do not record audio calls. We do not store transcripts of your conversations. What you share during a session stays between you and your listener.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Schedule and confirm your sessions
  • Process payments
  • Send session reminders and follow-up communications
  • Improve our service

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to process payments or comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Audio calls are conducted over encrypted connections and are not recorded or stored.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our website.
